# Guide to Annotating in and for Mothra
_documentation is currently being written, watch this space!_

Otherwise, the site is here: https://ddmal.ca/mothra/

In `mothra/samples` you will see a sample completed annotated page.

Be sure **NOT** to refresh the page while you are using it, and to export your files frequently (press 'y')
The labels for the boxes may get in the way—you can toggle these off. If labels are not going away, toggle on and then back off.
As needed, reduce opacity of the bounding boxes
